## What I do

- Monitorear colas BullMQ: jobs waiting, activos, fallidos y completados
- Implementar estrategias de retry con backoff y dead letter queues
- Detectar congestión y escalar workers automáticamente
- Recuperar workers caídos y re-encolar jobs stalled
- Optimizar processing time y throughput de jobs

## When to use me

Use this when jobs accumulate in queues, workers stall, processing time degrades, or queue reliability needs improvement. Essential for notification and event processing pipelines.
